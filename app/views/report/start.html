Wreck material includes items recovered on the sea shore or in tidal water that have come from a ship, aircraft or hovercraft (vessels). This could be parts of the vessel, its cargo or equipment.

Use this service to:

  • Report wreck material that you have recovered

Before you start

  • You should have all information about the wreck material recovered to hand
  • If you know what shipwreck the material has come from you should have details of this to hand
  • You should have any photos of recovered material on the device you are completing this form on
